About the Owner

One James Bond superfan has turned his passion for all-things-007 into a full-fledged tourist attraction. The James Bond Museum in the Swedish city of Nybro has everything to make any 007 fan swoon. James Bond Gunnar Schäfer (yes, that is the name of the founder of this museum) has amassed a collection of over 60,000 items.

(The owner James Bond Gunnar Schäfer)

Our Exhibitions

The 007 museum is devoted to celebrating the story of Ian Fleming’s James Bond 007. To bring the adventures of Bond to life, our exhibits showcase a plethora of familiar memorabilia from across all 24 of the released movies. Our collection includes a stunning range of vehicles, be it air, land or sea. You can experience flying like bond over Costa, in our new helicopter or how about checking out our garage where we have an Aston Martin branded with custom JB 007 license plates and even the actual Cessna 172 used in three of the Bond films.


From spy training to props used in filming, we offer something for everyone. Experience all this and immerse yourself in the world of her majesty's special agent with a visit to our spectacular show rooms.
